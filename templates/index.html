Our Story

A bakery born from passion

Since opening our doors in 1998, Frosted Dreams has been Coimbatore's most beloved artisan bakery. We wake at 3 AM so you can wake up to the smell of warm bread.

Every croissant is laminated by hand. Every cake is frosted with care. Every loaf is shaped by bakers who have dedicated their lives to this craft.
